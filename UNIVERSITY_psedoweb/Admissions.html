Undergraduate Admissions

Explore our diverse undergraduate programs and find your path to success. We offer a wide range of majors, minors, and specializations to suit your academic interests.

  • Minimum High School GPA: 3.0 (on a 4.0 scale)
  • Standardized Tests: SAT or ACT scores are required.
  • Application Fee: $60
  • Required Documents:
    • Official High School Transcripts
    • Letters of Recommendation (2)
    • Personal Essay

For more detailed information about undergraduate admissions, including program-specific requirements, please visit our dedicated Undergraduate Admissions page.

Graduate Admissions

Take your education to the next level with our challenging and rewarding graduate programs. We provide advanced learning opportunities and cutting-edge research experiences.

  • Minimum Bachelor's Degree GPA: 3.25
  • Standardized Tests: GRE or GMAT scores (program-specific)
  • Application Fee: $75
  • Required Documents:
    • Official Transcripts from all Universities Attended
    • Statement of Purpose
    • Letters of Recommendation (3)
    • Curriculum Vitae (CV) or Resume

Learn more about our graduate programs and their specific admission requirements on our Graduate Admissions page.

International Admissions

We welcome students from around the globe to our vibrant academic community. We are committed to providing support and resources to help you through the application process.

  • English Language Proficiency: TOEFL or IELTS scores
  • Visa Requirements: F-1 or J-1 visa eligibility
  • Transcript Evaluation: Transcripts must be evaluated by an approved agency.
  • Financial Documentation: Proof of sufficient funds to cover tuition and living expenses.

Find detailed information about applying as an international student, including visa requirements and accepted English language proficiency tests, on our International Admissions page.

Financial Aid

We believe that a high-quality education should be accessible to all qualified students. We offer a variety of financial aid options, including scholarships, grants, and loans.

  • Types of Aid: Merit-based scholarships, need-based grants, federal and private loans.
  • Application Process: Complete the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA).
  • Eligibility: Varies depending on the type of aid.

Explore our financial aid options and learn how to apply on our Financial Aid page.
